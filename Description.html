CampusLink: Your VIT Campus Delivery Network

Post a quick request — a fellow student walking by will pick it up and deliver it to you. Small tip. Big convenience.

Features

  • Smart Pricing System — Pricing depends on distance to be covered; integrated with maps for accurate charges.
  • Rating & Safety System — Users can rate delivery peers; repeated valid complaints can block a number.
  • Delivery Contact — The phone number of the person bringing your food is displayed for coordination.
  • In-App Chat — Communicate smoothly with the delivery peer without leaving the app.
  • Priority Orders — Frequent customers and highly rated delivery peers receive priority matching.
  • Outlet Integration — Seamless integration with popular campus outlets like One Food and DC Cafe.
  • Efficient Matching — Fast and reliable matching and delivery process to minimize wait time.

Advantages

  • Saves time and effort for busy students
  • Provides easy pocket money opportunities
  • Fosters community support and collaboration
  • Reduces overcrowding at outlets during peak hours
  • Encourages efficient use of student movement across campus

Future Prospects

  • Expansion beyond food to stationery, essentials, and courier services
  • Partnerships with campus outlets for exclusive discounts
  • AI-based matching for faster request fulfillment
  • Loyalty rewards program for frequent users
  • Scaling to other universities with similar campus structures

Revenue Model

  • Small platform fee (₹2–3) on each transaction
  • Collaborations with One Food, DC Cafe, and campus stores
  • Premium subscription for frequent requesters
  • Sponsored promotions with popular outlets
  • High earning potential for student deliverers (₹50–100/day)
